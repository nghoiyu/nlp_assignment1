    3. Dr Lau Teik Soon asked the Minister for Communications and Minister for Labour whether he will consider (a) an extension of the Singapore Bus Service concession passes to those Singapore citizens between 55 and 60 years old; and (b) the removal of the restricted use of the concession passes which presently can be used only during off-peak periods.

Column: 1584



     The Minister for Communications and Minister for Labour (Mr Ong Teng Cheong): Mr Speaker, Sir, SBS has to draw a line somewhere when giving concessions to senior citizens. Sixty years and above makes sense as many of those who are between 55 and 60 years old can be expected to be economically active in present-day circumstances.     The purpose of the concession is to encourage our senior citizens to lead a more active social life.
