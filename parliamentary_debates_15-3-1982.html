I am sure Members of the House are well aware of the measures being taken by the Ministry of Education in this direction.

    For the SAF, this remark of the First Deputy Prime Minister should not be interpreted to mean that theft is more prevalent among our national servicemen than among similar groups of young men elsewhere.

    Every year, 20,000 or so young men of 18 years of age are recruited into national service. They come from different walks of life. Some are fresh from school and eager to go for further studies.
